The Merkantile is a neighborhood shopping center prominently located at the corner of Highway 246 and Alamo Pintado Rd in the heart of the Santa Ynez Valley. The center serves as a hub for local commerce and serves the communities of Solvang, Santa Ynez, Ballard, Los Olivos, Buellton, and Los Alamos.
